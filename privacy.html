Privacy Policy

Client-Side Processing

Important: All image processing on Subedit is performed entirely in your browser. We do not store, collect, or transmit your images to any server.

When you use our tools:

  • Your files never leave your device
  • Processing happens locally in your web browser
  • No data is saved on our servers
  • Your privacy is completely preserved

How Our Tools Work

Our image editing tools use client-side JavaScript technologies to process your images:

  • File processing occurs entirely in your browser's memory
  • Edited files are downloaded directly to your device
  • Once you close the browser tab, all temporary data is automatically cleared
  • No cookies or local storage are used to store your images

Third-Party Services

The only exception to our client-side processing is our background removal tool, which requires the use of the remove.bg API. When using this specific tool:

  • Your image is temporarily transmitted to remove.bg's servers for processing
  • The transmission is secured using HTTPS encryption
  • The image is immediately deleted from their servers after processing
  • You will be clearly notified before using this feature

Analytics and Advertising

We use Google Analytics and AdSense to improve our service and maintain free access:

  • These services collect anonymous usage data
  • No personal information is collected
  • No information about your images is shared
  • You can opt-out using browser settings or ad blockers
