Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes, our PDF password remover is completely free to use with no hidden charges or limitations.

Will my files be uploaded to your servers?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your PDF files and passwords never leave your computer.

What if I don't know the password?

This tool requires you to know the current password of the PDF. We do not support cracking or bypassing unknown passwords.

What type of password protection can be removed?

This tool can remove user/open passwords (the one you need to open the PDF). It cannot remove owner/permission passwords that restrict editing or printing.

Are there any file size limitations?

Since all processing happens in your browser, the maximum file size depends on your device's memory. Generally, files up to 100MB should work fine.
